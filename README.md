# Unlocking-Behavioral-Intelligence-in-Airline-Loyalty-Programs

– Analyzed loyalty program data of 16,000+ airline members to find customer behavior, engagement patterns and retention opportunities.
– Studied airline loyalty programme of Air Canada, Emirates, Air India, Indigo and Singapore Airline from their website and several articles on Airline loyalty programme.
– Performed churn prediction analysis using inactivity periods, cancellation records and loyalty point redemption
behavior to identify high risk members.
– Built a framework to identify high value customers by combining flight frequency, travel distance, number of active months and reward redemption behavior.
– Explored and analysed the relationship between behavioral signals (flight frequency, redemption habits and seasonal activity) and demographic factors (salary band, education, marital status and card tier).
– Transformed raw airline data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, analysis and visualization
using Excel.
– Proposed data driven retention and loyalty enhancement strategies to improve customer engagement and long term program value.
